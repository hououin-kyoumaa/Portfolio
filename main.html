Hi my name is Harkeerat you can call me Hark. I am currently studying at SFU in the field of interactive systems, and yep you guessed it, i love games!

While there are many aspects to a game and individuals play for a variety of reasons, having solid mechanics and an interesting story are the most important in my opinion. A goofy bizarre idea with some interesting mechanics has the potential to become an addictive game.

I am familiar with the entire process of game development from the initial conceptual stages to the final product including playtesting and refinement. I have had the pleasure to work with various different teams and individuals and hopefully I will be able to work with you one day if you are looking to hire. If you stumbled here accidently you might as well play some of the games.
