I am a third-year Interactive arts and technology student at Simon Fraser University. My area of research includes issues revolving around games. Particularly I want to discern to what extent we should trust social realistic games such as America's Army as a viable knowledge medium. Data storage capacity is constantly expanding. Going from 4.4 MB in 1957 to and easily surpassing three terabytes in recent years (Computers & the Internet, n.d). With the increasing data capabilities, is it reliable to trust in increasing realistic games? As a game developer, I think it is important to understand the issues surrounding games and the potential implications of the games we create. My research seeks to understand the reliability of America's Army as an accurate depiction of warfare.
